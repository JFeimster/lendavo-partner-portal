# 🗣️ STRUCTURAL CONSULTATION PROTOCOL (Partner Script)
**Project:** Lendavo Partner Portal
**Document Purpose:** The authorized verbal framework for introducing the Lendavo Diagnostic to clients.
**Usage:** To be utilized during client discovery calls, tax strategy sessions, or quarterly reviews.

---

## 1. THE PIVOT (The Transition to Funding)
*Do not bring up funding out of nowhere. Use this exact pivot when a client mentions growth, capital needs, or cash flow concerns.*

**Partner:** "Before we look at external capital or growth projections, we need to have a structural conversation. Are you currently using your personal Social Security Number to guarantee your business credit cards or vendor terms?"

*(Client usually says "Yes" or "I think so")*

**Partner:** "We need to fix that immediately. In the current economic climate, traditional algorithms are rejecting about 54% of all business applications. The ones they *do* approve require absolute personal liability—meaning if the market shifts, your family’s home and savings are the collateral. I am not comfortable letting you operate with that level of exposure."

---

## 2. THE DIAGNOSTIC DEPLOYMENT (The "Ask")
*Position the Lendavo link as an exclusive internal tool, not an affiliate offer.*

**Partner:**
"Instead of guessing what the banks will say, I’m going to run your entity through our new Structural Diagnostic protocol. It’s an AI tool that scans the exact same bureau data the banks use. 

In about 60 seconds, it will identify any 'Invisible Landmines'—like address mismatches or missing 411 listings—that trigger auto-rejections. More importantly, it gives us the blueprint to decouple your personal SSN from your corporate EIN, so the business can secure capital on its own merit.

I'm sending you my secure link right now. I need you to fill this out before we proceed with the rest of our strategy."

---

## 3. ELITE OBJECTION HANDLING
*Clients will have knee-jerk reactions based on their experiences with traditional banks. Handle them clinically.*

**Client Objection 1: "Will this hurt my personal credit score?"**
**Partner Response:** "Zero impact. The diagnostic uses soft-data emulation. It mimics lender underwriting to generate your Funding Ready Score™ without ever placing a hard inquiry on your file."

**Client Objection 2: "My personal credit is over 800, I can just use that."**
**Partner Response:** "Your personal score is excellent, but relying on it is the exact trap we are avoiding. If you leverage your personal credit to fund a $100k business expansion, your personal debt-to-income ratio spikes. Your personal score will tank, and you won't be able to buy a home or car for your family. Our goal is entity independence. We are building a standalone corporate identity."

**Client Objection 3: "Is this just credit repair?"**
**Partner Response:** "No. Credit repair is reactionary. This is structural architecture. We aren't disputing old personal mistakes; we are engineering a corporate financial identity from the ground up."

---

## 4. THE FOLLOW-UP (Post-Scan)
*Once the client completes the scan, the Lendavo fulfillment team takes over, but the partner should maintain authority.*

**Partner:** "I see your diagnostic is complete. Our structural fulfillment team is going to review your Funding Ready Score™ and walk you through the exact steps to optimize your entity and secure your capital matches. Follow their protocol exactly, and let me know when your first EIN-only credit line is approved."
